batteries catergory: Batteries Household batteries of any size including battery packs from electrical items are suitable for recycling. Batteries should be brought to a Civic Amenity Site or lots of shops and supermarkets who sell batteries will have a collection point Check the Waste Services Locator on this site for further details. When you recycle anything with a plug or a battery you are helping to reduce the amount of waste going to landfill. Batteries generally contain hazardous components and therefore need to be disposed of correctly.
